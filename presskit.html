The Pitch

Trust no one. In this PSX-inspired horror game, anomalies are hiding among your inmates. It is your job to expose them before you accidentally deploy them into the world. Can you tell the difference between a human and an anomaly?

About The Game

The Facility is a psychological horror strategy game with a retro PSX aesthetic. Set in a world on the brink of collapse, you play as the newly appointed warden of Facility 61. Your job is to manage a rehabilitation center for subjects required by the State for dangerous reconstruction missions.

However, not everyone in your custody is human. Hidden among the inmates are Anomalies—mimics capable of disguising themselves as humans. Your survival depends on your ability to identify and contain these threats before they infiltrate the State's workforce.

Gameplay Overview

Your goal is to maintain the facility's order, satisfy the State's daily quotas for manpower, and prevent Anomalies from infiltrating the State's workforce. Sending an Anomaly on a mission doesn't just waste a slot; it results in the sabotage of critical operations and severe penalties from the State.

Core Features & Mechanics

Daily Loop

Receive Orders

Every morning, you receive instructions via fax from the State. You must analyze these requests to determine how many workers are needed and for what specific tasks.

Interrogation

You must interact with inmates face-to-face. Use the dialogue system to question them, inspect their documents for discrepancies, and observe their behavior for physical signs of corruption.

Selection

You decide which inmates to deploy. Sending a human to a death trap wastes valuable resources, but deploying an Anomaly will result in sabotage and mission failure.

Night Events

The day doesn't end with the deployment. Based on your choices, scripted night events may occur. Surviving the night is the only way to reach the next morning.

Anomaly Detection

Investigate inmate files, cross-reference their stories, and look for visual distortions. If you fail to identify an Anomaly and send them on an assignment, the State will hold you responsible for the consequences.

Paranoia is your only friend. Can you endure the nightmare of Facility 61?
